<h1 align=center>Internet Terms</h1>
<HR width=75%align=center color=red>
<DL>
<DT>client:
 <DD>Any computer on the networks that requests services from another computer on the
Internet.
<DT>Server:
 <DD>Any computer that receives requests from client computers,processes and sends the
output.
<DT>Webpage:
 <DD>:Any digital page/documents hosted on Internet(i.e.,accesible via Internet.)
<DT>Web Browser:
 <DD>A program or software on aclient computer,that retrieves information from the web
server.
